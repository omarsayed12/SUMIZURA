sumizura gmbh

is a dynamic Real-estate company that provides Architecture, Engineering and construction management services for commercial, institutional and residential projects.

Through our offices overseas (Frankfurt, Paris and Cairo) we offer design services, operations and facilities management expertise including project and construction management services.

Our team of professionals is dedicated in providing our clients with value added services, further associating our company’s identity with excellence.
